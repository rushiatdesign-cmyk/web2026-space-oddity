# 04 — Sticker Sheet ✨

**Your job: write the CSS. The HTML is done.**

A sheet of stickers, all different sizes. Open `goal.png` for the target:

- The stickers flow into **centered rows** that **wrap** when they run out of room, with even gaps.
- The wide **banner sticker** (`★ CERTIFIED FLEXBOX PILOT ★`) grows to eat the leftover space on its row.

Open `index.html` with Live Server, work through the TODOs in `style.css`, and refresh as you go.

Then the important part: **resize your browser window**, wide to narrow and back. Watch the stickers reflow into new rows all by themselves. You didn't write a single media query — that reflow is free, and it's your first taste of responsive design.

**Concepts:** `flex-wrap`, `gap`, `justify-content: center`, `align-items`, `flex-grow`.
