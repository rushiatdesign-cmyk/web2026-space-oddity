Exercise 07 · Three drills

Katas 🥋

A kata is a small movement you repeat until it's automatic. Three tiny layouts, no visual polish expected — these are drills, not portfolio pieces. Do each in its own file. Froggy levels 1–13 cover everything here, so revisiting the game counts as solving.

  • Kata 1 — The Navbar: name on the left, three links on the right.
  • Kata 2 — The Centered Card: a card centered horizontally and vertically in a full-height section.
  • Kata 3 — The Photo Row: three images sharing a row evenly, with gaps, that don't overflow.
